About Steve


Stephen D. Tarr, MSM, CAP, is uniquely qualified to assist your organization with nonprofit management and counsel for creating and sustaining high-impact giving programs.

As the lead development officer for a major nonprofit educational institution for over 15 years, Steve created a highly respected and robust philanthropy program.  During his tenure, the institution’s consolidated assets moved from approximately $6 million to over $60 million.

Working with corporate and private foundations, companies and individuals on annual fund, restricted as well as deferred gifts, Steve has made hundreds of presentations leading to successful asks. He offers exceptional strategic advice on identifying, cultivating and achieving meaningful gifts. He can also help nonprofit organizations select partners that best understand the theme of a campaign, milestone celebrations and proper gift acknowledgment. Another key element is helping navigate the "seat at the table" for donors, attorneys, accountants and others when it comes to more complex gifts.

Steve’s years of experience and expertise along with his strong credentials and education in charitable giving can provide you with the tools you need to achieve your fundraising goals and objectives.
